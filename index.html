Who We Are

Boost your interview prepration with the power of our AI-driven interview tool.

AIMockPrep bridges traditional methods with innovative technology. Using artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and machine learning, we create a virtual robot that simulates real interview scenarios, offering a realistic and interactive experience. The robot evaluates candidate responses, analyzes performance, and provides personalized feedback, making it a valuable tool for both job seekers and educational institutions..

Process

Effortless, Private & Personalized Interview Practice

  • Fill out initial information

    Fill out information such as job description, interview type, language.

  • Practice interviews as many times as you want

    Practise multiple live iterations of your interview by talking with our AI and get instantaneous feedback.

  • Data stays private

    The information you provide such as the job description, will remain private. We do not store any of this data on our servers.
